  • Works in 30-60 minutes

Important Medical Information

When to Consult Your Doctor

While we provide general information, always consult a licensed healthcare provider before starting ED medication, especially if you:

  • Have heart or cardiovascular conditions
  • Take nitrate medications for chest pain
  • Have had a recent heart attack or stroke
  • Have liver or kidney problems
  • Have blood pressure issues
  • Have bleeding disorders
  • Have eye problems (retinitis pigmentosa)
  • Take multiple medications

General Safety Guidelines

  • Follow prescribed dosage instructions
  • Do not exceed one dose per 24 hours
  • Avoid excessive alcohol consumption
  • Do not mix with recreational drugs
  • Seek medical help if erection lasts over 4 hours
  • Stop use and seek help for sudden vision or hearing loss
