What is Generic Medication?

Generic medications contain the same active ingredients as their branded counterparts and undergo rigorous FDA testing to ensure they meet the same quality and safety standards. Generic Sildenafil must demonstrate bioequivalence to Viagra, meaning it works in the same way and produces the same clinical results. The lower price reflects savings on marketing and development costs, not a difference in quality.

How Generic Sildenafil Works

Sildenafil is a PDE5 inhibitor that increases blood flow to the penis by relaxing blood vessel walls. When sexually aroused, this enhanced blood flow helps achieve and maintain an erection. The medication typically takes effect within 30-60 minutes and lasts for 4-6 hours. Sexual stimulation is required for the medication to work.

Dosage and Usage

The recommended starting dose is 50mg, taken approximately one hour before sexual activity. Based on effectiveness and side effects, the dose may be adjusted to 100mg or reduced to 25mg. Take no more than one dose per 24-hour period. For best results, take on an empty stomach or after a light meal.

Important: Sildenafil requires sexual stimulation to work. It does not increase sexual desire but helps achieve an erection when aroused.

Who Can Take Sildenafil?

Generic Sildenafil is suitable for men over 18 with erectile dysfunction. However, consult a healthcare provider before use if you have heart problems, recent heart attack or stroke, blood pressure issues, liver or kidney disease, or take nitrate medications.

Common Side Effects

Side effects are typically mild and may include headache, facial flushing, indigestion, nasal congestion, and visual changes (blue tint). These effects are temporary and usually subside within a few hours. Seek immediate medical attention for erections lasting more than 4 hours, sudden vision loss, or chest pain.
