About Generic Sildenafil

Generic Sildenafil contains the same active ingredient as branded Viagra but at a fraction of the cost. Once Pfizer's patent expired, other pharmaceutical companies were able to produce bioequivalent versions of Sildenafil Citrate, offering the same effectiveness and safety profile at more affordable prices.

What is Generic Medication?

Generic medications contain the same active ingredients as their branded counterparts and undergo rigorous testing to ensure they meet the same quality and safety standards. Generic Sildenafil must demonstrate bioequivalence to Viagra, meaning it works in the same way and produces the same clinical results.

How Generic Sildenafil Works

Sildenafil is a PDE5 inhibitor that increases blood flow to the penis by relaxing blood vessel walls. When sexually aroused, this enhanced blood flow helps achieve and maintain an erection. The medication typically takes effect within 30-60 minutes and lasts for 4-6 hours.

Who Can Take Sildenafil?

Generic Sildenafil is suitable for men over 18 with erectile dysfunction. Consult a healthcare provider before use if you:

  • Have heart problems or cardiovascular disease
  • Have had a recent heart attack or stroke
  • Have low or high blood pressure
  • Have liver or kidney disease
  • Take nitrate medications for chest pain
  • Take alpha-blockers for prostate problems

Dosage and Usage

The recommended starting dose is 50mg, taken approximately one hour before sexual activity. Based on effectiveness and side effects, the dose may be adjusted to 100mg or reduced to 25mg. Take no more than one dose per 24-hour period.

Important: Sildenafil requires sexual stimulation to work. It does not increase sexual desire but helps achieve an erection when aroused.

Common Side Effects

Most men tolerate Sildenafil well. Common side effects include:

  • Headache
  • Facial flushing
  • Indigestion or heartburn
  • Nasal congestion
  • Dizziness
  • Visual disturbances (blue tinge, rare)

These effects are typically mild and resolve within a few hours. Seek immediate medical help if you experience chest pain, vision loss, or an erection lasting more than 4 hours.

Tips for Best Results

  • Take on an empty stomach for faster absorption
  • Avoid excessive alcohol consumption
  • Allow 30-60 minutes before sexual activity
  • Sexual stimulation is necessary for the medication to work
  • Do not take with grapefruit juice
