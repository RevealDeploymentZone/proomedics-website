Understanding Erectile Dysfunction

Erectile dysfunction (ED) affects millions of men worldwide. It is a common condition characterized by the inability to achieve or maintain an erection sufficient for sexual activity. ED can be caused by various factors including stress, anxiety, medical conditions, or lifestyle factors.

Modern ED medications work by increasing blood flow to the penis, helping achieve and maintain an erection when sexually stimulated. These treatments are highly effective and have helped millions of men regain confidence and improve their quality of life.
