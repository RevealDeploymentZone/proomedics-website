Who Can Use Kamagra?

Kamagra is suitable for adult men over 18 experiencing erectile dysfunction. Medical consultation recommended before use. Not suitable if you:

  • Have serious heart or cardiovascular conditions
  • Had recent heart attack or stroke (within 6 months)
  • Have low blood pressure or uncontrolled hypertension
  • Have severe liver or kidney disease
  • Take nitrate medications for chest pain
  • Take riociguat for pulmonary hypertension
  • Are allergic to Sildenafil

Dosage and Administration

Standard dose is one 100mg tablet taken approximately 30-60 minutes before anticipated sexual activity. Do not exceed one dose per 24-hour period. Can be taken with or without food, though high-fat meals may delay absorption. Sexual stimulation is required for the medication to work.

Possible Side Effects

Common side effects (similar to all Sildenafil products):

  • Headache - most common, usually mild
  • Facial flushing - temporary redness
  • Nasal congestion - stuffy nose
  • Indigestion - mild stomach discomfort
  • Dizziness - especially when standing quickly
  • Visual disturbances - rare, temporary color tinge

Most side effects are mild and resolve within hours. Seek immediate medical attention if you experience an erection lasting over 4 hours, sudden vision or hearing loss, or chest pain.
