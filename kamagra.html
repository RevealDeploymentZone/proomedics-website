About Kamagra

Kamagra is a cost-effective erectile dysfunction treatment containing the same active ingredient as Viagra - Sildenafil Citrate. Manufactured by Ajanta Pharma, Kamagra provides an affordable alternative for men seeking effective ED treatment without the premium brand price.

How Kamagra Works

Kamagra contains 100mg of Sildenafil Citrate, a PDE5 inhibitor that works by relaxing blood vessels in the penis and increasing blood flow during sexual arousal. The medication typically begins working within 30-60 minutes and remains effective for 4-6 hours, providing ample time for sexual activity.

Kamagra vs Viagra

Kamagra and Viagra contain the same active ingredient (Sildenafil Citrate) and work in the same way. The main differences are:

  • Price: Kamagra is significantly more affordable
  • Manufacturer: Viagra by Pfizer, Kamagra by Ajanta Pharma
  • Availability: Kamagra available in tablets and oral jelly
  • Effectiveness: Both contain 100mg Sildenafil with equivalent results

Forms of Kamagra

Kamagra Tablets: Standard tablet form, taken with water 30-60 minutes before sexual activity.

Kamagra Oral Jelly: Convenient gel sachets in various flavors, faster absorption, ideal for those who have difficulty swallowing tablets.

Kamagra Gold: Premium formulation with enhanced bioavailability for faster onset.

Who Can Use Kamagra?

Kamagra is suitable for adult men over 18 experiencing erectile dysfunction. Not recommended for those with:

  • Serious heart or cardiovascular conditions
  • Recent stroke or heart attack
  • Low or uncontrolled high blood pressure
  • Severe liver or kidney disease
  • Currently taking nitrate medications

Dosage Instructions

The standard dose is one 100mg tablet taken approximately one hour before sexual activity. Do not take more than one dose within 24 hours. Kamagra can be taken with or without food, though high-fat meals may delay absorption.

Kamagra is generally well-tolerated. Potential side effects include:

  • Headache
  • Facial flushing
  • Nasal congestion
  • Indigestion

Most side effects are mild and temporary. Consult a doctor if side effects persist or worsen.
