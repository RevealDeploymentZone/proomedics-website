About Spedra

Spedra is the newest prescription medication for erectile dysfunction, containing the active ingredient Avanafil. Approved for use in Europe in 2013, Spedra represents the latest advancement in ED treatment technology. It is the fastest-acting ED medication available, with some men experiencing results in as little as 15 minutes.

How Spedra Works

Avanafil, the active ingredient in Spedra, is a highly selective PDE5 inhibitor specifically designed for rapid onset and clearance. It works by increasing blood flow to the penis during sexual arousal, enabling achievement and maintenance of an erection. The medication is metabolized quickly, which contributes to both its fast action and reduced side effect profile.

Who Can Take Spedra?

Spedra is suitable for adult men over 18 with erectile dysfunction. Consult your doctor before use if you:

  • Have heart conditions or cardiovascular disease
  • Have had a recent heart attack or stroke
  • Have angina or chest pain
  • Have low or uncontrolled high blood pressure
  • Have severe liver impairment
  • Take nitrate medications
  • Have anatomical deformation of the penis
  • Have conditions predisposing to priapism

Dosage and Administration

The recommended starting dose is 100mg, taken approximately 15-30 minutes before sexual activity. Based on individual response and tolerance, the dose may be increased to 200mg or decreased to 50mg. Do not take more than one dose per 24 hours.

Important: Spedra can be taken with or without food. Unlike some ED medications, moderate amounts of food do not significantly affect its absorption.

Possible Side Effects

Spedra is generally well tolerated. Common side effects may include:

  • Headache (most common, usually mild)
  • Facial flushing
  • Nasal congestion
  • Back pain (rare)
  • Dizziness

Studies show Spedra has a lower incidence of side effects compared to older ED medications. Most side effects are mild and temporary. Seek immediate medical attention if you experience vision changes, chest pain, or an erection lasting more than 4 hours.

Safety Information

  • Do not combine with nitrate medications
  • Avoid recreational drugs containing nitrites (poppers)
  • Use caution with alpha-blockers
  • Moderate alcohol consumption is acceptable
  • May cause dizziness - be careful driving initially
  • Not for use by women or individuals under 18
