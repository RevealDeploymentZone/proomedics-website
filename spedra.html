About Spedra (Avanafil)

Spedra is the newest prescription medication for erectile dysfunction, containing the active ingredient Avanafil. Approved by the FDA in 2012, Spedra represents the latest advancement in ED treatment technology. It is the fastest-acting ED medication available, with some men experiencing results in as little as 15 minutes. Spedra offers improved selectivity and a better side effect profile compared to older ED medications.

How Spedra Works

Avanafil, the active ingredient in Spedra, is a highly selective PDE5 inhibitor specifically designed for rapid onset and clearance. It works by increasing blood flow to the penis during sexual arousal, enabling achievement and maintenance of an erection. The medication is metabolized quickly, which contributes to both its fast action and reduced side effect profile. Sexual stimulation is required for Spedra to be effective.

Dosage and Administration

The recommended starting dose is 100mg, taken approximately 15-30 minutes before sexual activity. Based on effectiveness and tolerance, the dose may be increased to 200mg. Do not exceed one dose per 24 hours. Spedra can be taken with or without food. Unlike some other ED medications, high-fat meals do not significantly impact its effectiveness.

Clinical Effectiveness

Clinical trials have demonstrated Spedra's effectiveness:

  • 60-70% of men achieve successful intercourse

  • Effective across different severities of ED

  • Works for men with diabetes and other conditions
  • Maintains effectiveness with regular use

Who Can Take Spedra?

Spedra is suitable for adult men experiencing erectile dysfunction. However, consult your healthcare provider before use if you have cardiovascular conditions, recent heart attack or stroke, blood pressure issues, liver or kidney problems, or take certain medications. Spedra should not be taken with nitrate medications.

Side Effects

Common side effects of Spedra include headache, facial flushing, nasal congestion, and back pain. Due to its high selectivity, Spedra generally has a more favorable side effect profile than older ED medications. Side effects are typically mild and temporary. Seek immediate medical attention for erections lasting more than 4 hours, sudden vision or hearing loss, or chest pain.
