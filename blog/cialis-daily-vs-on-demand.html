Cialis Daily vs On-Demand: Which Should You Choose?

By Promedic Medical Team  |  July 19, 2026  |  8 min read

Cialis (tadalafil) is unique among ED medications in offering two distinct approaches: as-needed (10mg or 20mg) and daily low-dose (2.5mg or 5mg every day). Choosing between them depends on your lifestyle and how frequently you are sexually active.

Key Takeaway: Cialis daily suits men sexually active 2+ times per week who want complete spontaneity. On-demand suits men with less frequent activity who prefer medication only when needed. Both are equally effective.

How Cialis On-Demand Works

The standard as-needed dose is 10mg, taken 30-45 minutes before activity. If insufficient, increase to 20mg. Cialis lasts up to 36 hours — take it Friday evening and it covers the weekend without another dose. Ideal for men sexually active less than twice a week.

How Cialis Daily Works

A small tablet (2.5mg or 5mg) taken every day at the same time builds a steady bloodstream concentration, making spontaneous erections possible at any time without planning. Per the Mayo Clinic, tadalafil daily also improves urinary symptoms in men with benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH).
