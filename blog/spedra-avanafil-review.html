Spedra (Avanafil): The Fastest-Acting ED Medication Reviewed

By Promedic Medical Team  |  July 17, 2026  |  8 min read

Spedra (avanafil) is the newest FDA-approved erectile dysfunction medication and arguably the most convenient. Where Viagra and Levitra require 30-60 minutes, Spedra can work in as little as 15 minutes. Here is a complete review.

Key Takeaway: Spedra has the fastest onset of any approved ED medication — 15-30 minutes — with a good safety profile and fewer food interactions. Excellent for men who want spontaneity without the planning required by older PDE5 inhibitors.

What is Spedra (Avanafil)?

Spedra contains avanafil and was FDA-approved in 2012. It is a fourth-generation PDE5 inhibitor with a more selective, faster-acting molecular structure than sildenafil or tadalafil. It comes in 50mg, 100mg, and 200mg tablets. Clinical trials reported in the Journal of Sexual Medicine showed onset in as little as 15 minutes in men with mild to moderate ED.

Food and Alcohol Interactions

Like Levitra, Spedra is minimally affected by food — a significant advantage over Viagra. You can take it with or without a meal. Moderate alcohol (1-2 drinks) is generally compatible, though heavy drinking should be avoided with any ED medication.

Side Effects

Avanafil shares common PDE5 inhibitor side effects (headache, flushing, nasal congestion) but clinical data suggests these are generally milder and less frequent than sildenafil, per the European Medicines Agency. Notably lower risk of vision changes (associated with sildenafil) and back pain (associated with tadalafil).

Who is Spedra Best For?

  • Men who want the fastest onset for maximum spontaneity
  • Men who have experienced side effects on Viagra or Cialis
  • Men who frequently eat before taking their medication
  • Men who have tried other PDE5 inhibitors without full satisfaction
