About Levitra

Levitra is a prescription medication containing Vardenafil, developed by Bayer for treating erectile dysfunction. Known for its rapid onset of action, Levitra typically works within 25-60 minutes and remains effective for 4-5 hours. It offers a reliable alternative for men who may not respond well to other ED medications.

How Levitra Works

Vardenafil, the active ingredient in Levitra, is a selective PDE5 inhibitor that enhances erectile function by increasing blood flow to the penis during sexual stimulation. The medication works by blocking the enzyme phosphodiesterase type 5, allowing smooth muscle relaxation and improved blood circulation.

Levitra vs Other ED Medications

Levitra vs Viagra:

  • Levitra may work faster (25 min vs 30-60 min)
  • Less affected by food intake
  • May be more effective for men with diabetes
  • Similar duration of action (4-5 hours)

Levitra vs Cialis:

  • Levitra has faster onset than Cialis
  • Cialis lasts longer (up to 36 hours)
  • Levitra more suitable for on-demand use
  • Both can be taken with food

Who Can Take Levitra?

Levitra is suitable for men over 18 experiencing erectile dysfunction. Consult your doctor before use if you:

  • Have cardiovascular conditions or heart problems
  • Have had a heart attack or stroke within the last 6 months
  • Have low or high blood pressure
  • Have liver or kidney problems
  • Have a history of priapism
  • Take nitrate medications or alpha-blockers
  • Have bleeding disorders or active peptic ulcers

Dosage and Administration

The recommended starting dose is 10mg, taken approximately 25-60 minutes before sexual activity. Based on efficacy and tolerance, the dose may be increased to 20mg or decreased to 5mg. Do not exceed one dose per 24 hours.

Tip: While Levitra can be taken with food, avoid high-fat meals as they may delay absorption and effectiveness.

Effectiveness

Clinical studies show that Levitra is highly effective:

  • 80% of men report improved erections
  • Effective across various causes of ED
  • Particularly beneficial for men with diabetes
  • Consistent results with repeated use
  • High satisfaction rates among users

Possible Side Effects

Common side effects of Levitra include:

  • Headache
  • Facial flushing
  • Stuffy or runny nose
  • Indigestion
  • Dizziness
  • Back pain (less common than with Cialis)

Side effects are generally mild and short-lived. Contact your doctor immediately if you experience sudden vision or hearing loss, chest pain, or an erection lasting more than 4 hours.

Important Safety Information

  • Do not take with nitrate medications
  • Avoid excessive alcohol consumption
  • Not for use with recreational drugs containing nitrites
  • May cause dizziness - be cautious when driving
  • Seek medical help for erections lasting over 4 hours
